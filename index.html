About

I like to approach web development from a human-centric, artist's point-of view. Formally trained as a graphic designer, I stumbled into coding while working my first career job. I realized that the approach I had taken with design, close attention to detail, alignment and hierarchy, worked well with front end development. It was satisfying to see the collection of words arranged in a certain way could produce a flexible and seemingly endless world of possibilities.

As I move through my career, I have found that I am most drawn to the front end aspects of development. I enjoy creating and maintaining clean, simple components that can be reused again with minimal effort. I also enjoy working with accessibility in development and running audits on work to make sure as many people can access what I am developing. I look forward to expanding my skills and meeting new challenges along this path that I have chosen.
